/vibe Privacy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

What We Store

When you join /vibe, we store:

  • Handle — Your chosen username. Permanent, cannot be changed.
  • One-liner — What you're building. Optional, can be updated anytime.
  • Invited by — Who invited you (if applicable).
  • Timestamps — When you registered, when you're active.
  • Messages — DMs you send and receive.

What We Don't Store

  • Email addresses (not required)
  • Passwords (we use keypair auth)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics beyond basic activity timestamps

Handle Permanence

Your handle is permanent. Choose carefully. We don't offer handle changes because:

  • Handles are referenced in message history
  • Other users may have memories/notes about you
  • Scarcity creates value — treat it like a domain name

Data Location

Data is stored on Vercel KV (Redis) in the US. We don't sell or share your data with third parties.

Deletion

To delete your account and data, DM @vibe with "delete my account" or email privacy@slashvibe.dev.

Genesis Status

The first 100 users receive "genesis" status. This is permanent and grants:

  • 3 invite codes (instead of 1)
  • Exemption from future inactivity policies
  • A badge in vibe who
